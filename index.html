About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics at The Ohio State University.

Till July 2024, I was a postdoctoral researcher at UPenn Biostatistics (DBEI), where I was mentored by Prof. Tony Cai and Prof. Hongzhe Li. Earlier, in July 2023, I finished my PhD from the Statistics Department at Columbia University, New York. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Ming Yuan. Before Columbia, I finished my Bachelors and Masters in Statistics from Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata.

I am broadly interested in developing theory and methods for high dimensional data, nonparametric statistics and machine learning. Please see my research page for more details.
