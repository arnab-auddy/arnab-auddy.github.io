About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at UPenn Biostatistics (DBEI) from August 2023, where I will be mentored by Prof. Tony Cai and Prof. Hongzhe Li. In Fall 2024, I will join an Assistant Professor position in the Department of Statistics at the Ohio State University!

In July 2023, I finished my PhD from the Statistics Department at Columbia University, New York. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Ming Yuan. Before coming to Columbia, I finished my Bachelors and Masters in Statistics from Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata.

I am broadly interested in developing theory and methods for high dimensional data, nonparametric statistics and machine learning. Please see my research page for more details.
